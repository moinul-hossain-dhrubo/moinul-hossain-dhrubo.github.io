My story

I used to hear from most of the elders saying that, when you grow up, you have to become a doctor or an engineer. Why, I asked? The answer would be like, they make a very good living, which is another way of saying that they earn a decent amount of money to be well-off, which makes sense. But, No one told me what interesting works an engineer do or what will the motivation behind becoming a doctor or an engineer. So, I delibarately chose not to become a doctor or an engineer as for that time, I thought serves me no purpose to aim for. I always thought, these are for the smarter people than me. While growing up, I started to notice the use of computer technology which really amazed me. I was intrigued and asked myself questions, such as, how a QR code works? or how can an smartwatch instantly transfer money to the receipent?

I first used ChatGPT in 2022, which at that time truly amazed me. A question instantly came to my mind: "How does this thing work? I need to learn this." It was only then I came to know about AI, NLP, and stuffs like that. After my graduation in January 2023, I first started learning programming on Kaggle and consequently started learning about Machine Learning and how those algorithms work. My elder brother is the one who constantly guided me on the path and I feel really honoured and proud to be a sibling of such a talented and humble human.


In October 2023, I joined MasterCourse to further sharpen my skills. Under the guidance of my mentor, I started building projects with my own idea, which really gave me the feeling of doing something for a purpose. When someone says, "Hey, I used your app. It's great", or maybe if someone gives me a feedback, these are the moments when I feel happy from the inside to know that it helped someone.


This is the continuation of my journey where I explore the depths of machine learning algorithms, AI models, and how they impact our world. I have always believed in the power of data, and I am constantly seeking to improve my skills and knowledge in this field. I am currently working on building AI models, analyzing large datasets, and contributing to various projects that involve data science, AI, and automation.
