My story

Wherever I see, there is technology. It always fascinated me how these things work. For instance, how can a QR code mean something? Or how does a smartwatch pay the bills instantly? I first used ChatGPT in 2022, which at that time truly amazed me. A question instantly came to my mind: "How does this thing work?" It was only then I came to know about AI, NLP, and stuff like that. After my graduation with a Bachelor of Arts in Music, I first started learning programming on Kaggle and consequently started learning about Machine Learning and how those algorithms work.

This is the continuation of my journey where I explore the depths of machine learning algorithms, AI models, and how they impact our world. I have always believed in the power of data, and I am constantly seeking to improve my skills and knowledge in this field. I am currently working on building AI models, analyzing large datasets, and contributing to various projects that involve data science, AI, and automation.
