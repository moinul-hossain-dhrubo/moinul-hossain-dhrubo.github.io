Why this Website?

As an introvert, I don't like bragging about myself. Actions speak Louder, right? ;) . But as I grow and meet new people, I realize the importance of sharing my journey in an easy and meaningful way. This website is my way of introducing myself, letting my work and passions speak for me. So here it is—my story.

An Overview

Being a learner by heart and a soldier by passion :p , I explored guitar playing, music studies, photography, programming, Artificial Intelligence, Chess, Physical training and more. Through this website, I’ll share the stories behind each journey—what drew me to them and how they shaped me. Beyond these, I’m deeply interested in philosophy, psychology, economics, politics, history, and self-development books. You might call me a "jack of all trades, master of none."—and I’m okay with that.
